SYNOPSIS

María José's life runs between college, always-distant parents, rugby training and adventures with her best friend Carlos.

Emotionally disconnected from her environment, one day she meets Javier and tries to start a relationship with him. But her efforts to have a normal life are all in vain. María José is carrying a secret: a pregnancy that everyone refuses to see.
